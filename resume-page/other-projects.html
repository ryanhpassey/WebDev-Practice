Much smaller in scale than my videogame, I have dabbled in a couple other projects as well. Namely:

  • Google Sheets custom AppScript functions
  • This webpage

I thought it would be fun to have a place where my friends and I could rank our favorite TV shows and other media, and then have Sheets average our scores together.

I wrote some custom script that reads the other sheets for entries, and prints the scores listed for identical items onto a master list. From there I can get the average of those scores and find what movies / games / albums are the most popular among my friend group.

Though I have no particular interest in web development, I designed and coded the HTML and CSS for this site as well.
